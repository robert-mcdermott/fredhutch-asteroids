# Fred Hutch Asteroids

Pilot the mighty Fred Hutch through a microscopic battlefield of rogue tumors, hostile oncoviruses, and suspiciously aggressive cell clusters. Fire T-cells, vaccines, radiation bursts, and other science-powered payloads as you break big tumors into smaller problems, collect research-grade powerups, and prove once again that teamwork, technology, and a well-aimed blast of immunotherapy can save the day. Join the fight today: https://fredhutch.gitlab.io/fredhutch-asteroids/
