# Fred Hutch Asteroids

Fred Hutch Asteroids is a browser-based arcade game inspired by the classic
asteroids formula and reimagined as a cancer research mission.

Pilot the Fred Hutch discovery craft through a microscopic battlefield of rogue
tumors, hostile oncoviruses, and fast-dividing cell clusters. Protect your R01
grant portfolio, collect translational research boosts, and deploy
science-powered therapies including killer T-cells, mRNA-guided vaccines,
radiation pulses, and chemo infusions.

Play the hosted version: <https://fredhutch.gitlab.io/fredhutch-asteroids/>

Support Fred Hutch Cancer Center: <https://www.fredhutch.org/en/ways-to-give.html>

## Gameplay

Your goal is to clear tumor waves while keeping the discovery craft funded and
intact.

- Tumors split into smaller fragments when destroyed.
- Oncoviruses enter periodically, move independently, and fire back.
- R01 Grants replace lives; each serious hit costs one grant.
- Powerups provide rapid fire, shields, triple-shot protocols, thruster boosts,
  mRNA-guided homing shots, radiation pulse charges, chemo infusion charges, and
  R01 bridge grants.
- Short science notes appear during play to connect the arcade action with
  cancer prevention, treatment, clinical trials, precision oncology,
  immunotherapy, supportive care, NIH-funded research, and philanthropy.
- The background includes subtle drifting DNA, protein, nucleotide, and marker
  motifs for a living microscopy feel.

## Controls

| Action | Keyboard |
| --- | --- |
| Thrust | `W` or `ArrowUp` |
| Rotate | `A` / `D` or `ArrowLeft` / `ArrowRight` |
| Fire T-cell shots | `Space` |
| Boost | `Shift` |
| Hold shield | `S` or `ArrowDown` |
| Chemo infusion | `C` |
| Radiation pulse | `P` |
| Pause or resume | `Esc` |
| Mute or unmute | `M` |

Touch controls appear automatically on small screens.

## Therapy Arsenal

- **Killer T-cells:** the default projectile.
- **mRNA-guided vaccine volley:** temporary homing shots that gently seek tumor
  and oncovirus targets.
- **Radiation pulse:** a charged expanding wave of ionizing energy.
- **Chemo infusion:** a charged lingering cytotoxic field that repeatedly damages
  nearby fast-dividing targets.
- **Protective shield:** a temporary defensive field that can absorb hits while
  held.
