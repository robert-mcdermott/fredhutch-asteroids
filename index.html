Fred Hutch Asteroids

Pilot the mighty Fred Hutch discovery craft through a microscopic battlefield of rogue tumors, hostile oncoviruses, and suspiciously aggressive cell clusters. Fire T-cells, vaccines, radiation bursts, and other science-powered payloads as you break big tumors into smaller problems, collect research-grade powerups, and prove once again that teamwork, technology, and a well-aimed blast of immunotherapy can save the day.

Therapy ArsenalKiller T-cells, mRNA guidance, radiation pulses, chemo bursts, and vaccine volleys.
Research PowerupsRapid fire, shield, triple shot, thruster boost, extra life, and homing vaccine mode.
Arcade LearningShort science notes appear as you clear waves, without turning the game into homework.
W / ↑ thrust, A/D or ←/→ rotate, Space fire, Shift boost, S / ↓ hold to shield, P radiation pulse ☢, Esc pause, M mute. Touch controls appear on small screens.
